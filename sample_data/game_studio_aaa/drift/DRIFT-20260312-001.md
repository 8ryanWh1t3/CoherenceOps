## Signal

Berlin's Revenue Analytics team detected a severe collapse in EU Average Revenue Per Paying User (ARPPU) during the W11 telemetry review cycle. EU ARPPU dropped from $14.50 (W10 average) to $8.90 (W11 rolling 7-day average as of 2026-03-12), representing a 38.6% decline in one week. This is the steepest single-week ARPPU decline in Eclipse Dominion's 18-month live-service history.

The collapse is a direct cascade from DRIFT-20260310-002 (EU DAU drop of 18%). However, the ARPPU decline is disproportionate to the DAU loss -- high-spending players ("whales," defined as ARPPU > $40/month) churned at 2.3x the rate of average players. The whale segment represented 8.2% of EU paying users but contributed 34% of EU MTX revenue. Whale churn rate accelerated from 1.1% weekly (baseline) to 4.8% weekly during W11.

Revenue run-rate extrapolation shows a $38M annualized shortfall against the FY2026 EU revenue plan if current ARPPU levels persist. The EU region contributes 28% of Eclipse Dominion's global revenue ($134M/yr at plan). The shortfall represents a 28.4% gap to the EU revenue target and a 7.9% gap to the global revenue target ($480M/yr).

## Impact

- **Revenue**: $38M annualized shortfall at current run-rate. Q2 EU revenue reforecast required -- original plan: $33.6M; current trajectory: $24.2M (-$9.4M in Q2 alone).
- **EBITDA**: At 68% gross margin, the revenue shortfall translates to -$25.8M annualized EBITDA impact. AetherForge's FY2026 EBITDA target of $96M is now at risk.
- **Whale retention**: 412 of the top-1000 EU spenders (>$100/month) have not made a purchase in the past 7 days. Historical data shows that whales who go 10+ days without a purchase have a 72% probability of permanent churn.
- **Investor relations**: AetherForge's Q1 earnings call is scheduled for 2026-04-15. The EU ARPPU collapse, if not reversed, will force a guidance revision.
- **Downstream cascade**: Compounds DRIFT-20260313-002 (sponsor renegotiation) as sponsors weigh engagement metrics alongside uptime.

## Root Cause

Three compounding factors drove the ARPPU collapse:

1. **Price sensitivity shock**: The Season 4 EU pricing increase (DLR-20260224-003, +22%) followed by the emergency rollback (DLR-20260309-001) created a "trust whiplash" effect. Players who spent at Season 4 prices felt exploited; players who waited felt vindicated in not spending.
2. **Whale exodus**: High-spending players are disproportionately sensitive to monetization controversy. The canon contradiction (DRIFT-20260311-001) was amplified in whale-heavy Discord communities (top-spender channels with 12,000 members).
3. **Refund cannibalization**: The $2.1M refund program (DLR-20260309-001) returned purchasing power to players but did not re-engage them. Only 11% of refund recipients made a new purchase within 7 days of refund receipt.

- **Downstream**: Contributes to [DRIFT-20260313-002](DRIFT-20260313-002.md) -- sponsor renegotiation (engagement metric degradation). Triggers EU revenue reforecast in [DRIFT-20260323-001](DRIFT-20260323-001.md) -- recovery metrics below target.

## Patch / Resolution

**Status: In Progress.** Multi-track recovery plan initiated:

1. **Whale re-engagement campaign** (Berlin, launched 2026-03-13): Personalized outreach to top-1000 EU spenders with exclusive cosmetic bundles and 15% loyalty discount. Target: re-engage 60% within 14 days. Current progress: 28% re-engaged as of 2026-03-19.
2. **Direct-purchase bundle optimization** (Berlin, in progress): A/B testing bundle configurations to maximize conversion among mid-tier spenders ($10-40/month). First results expected W13.
3. **Battle Pass value increase** (Montreal, scheduled 2026-03-20): Season 4.1 Battle Pass to include 30% more cosmetic content at the same price point. Goal: compensate for loot box removal revenue loss.
4. **Revenue reforecast** (LA Finance, due 2026-03-25): Updated EU revenue model incorporating observed churn elasticity and ARPPU recovery trajectory.

Full resolution requires sustained ARPPU recovery to >$12.00 over 4-6 weeks. Current trajectory suggests $11.20 by W15 (below target).
