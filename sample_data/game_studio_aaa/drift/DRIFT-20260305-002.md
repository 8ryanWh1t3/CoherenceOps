## Signal

The Sentiment Anomaly Index (SAI) -- a composite metric derived from Reddit NLP sentiment (30%), Twitter/X mention volume and sentiment (25%), Discord support ticket velocity (25%), and app store review sentiment (20%) -- spiked to 82 at 14:30 UTC on March 5. This exceeded the sponsor review threshold of 75 and represented the highest SAI reading in Eclipse Dominion's history, surpassing the previous peak of 68 during the Season 2 matchmaking overhaul controversy (October 2025).

The SAI component breakdown at peak:

| Component | Normal Range | March 5 Peak | Weight |
|---|---|---|---|
| Reddit NLP sentiment | 15-25 | 91 | 30% |
| Twitter/X volume + sentiment | 10-20 | 87 | 25% |
| Discord ticket velocity | 8-15 | 78 | 25% |
| App store review sentiment | 5-12 | 64 | 20% |

Reddit r/EclipseDominion experienced a surge of 3,400 negative-sentiment posts in 24 hours (baseline: 120/day). The top 20 posts by engagement were all criticism-focused, with a combined 142,000 upvotes. Twitter/X saw 218,000 mentions of #EclipseDominion in 24 hours (baseline: 12,000/day), with negative sentiment ratio of 73% (baseline: 18%). Discord support channels processed 2,800 tickets in 24 hours (baseline: 180/day). App store reviews on iOS and Android dropped from a 7-day rolling average of 4.2 stars to 2.1 stars, with 1,840 one-star reviews posted in 24 hours (baseline: 45/day).

The SAI remained above 75 for 11 hours (14:30 UTC March 5 to 01:30 UTC March 6) before declining to 68 following the community response actions from DRIFT-20260305-001.

## Impact

- **Sponsor contractual risk**: The SAI reading of 82 directly threatened sponsor agreements. OrionTel (clause 7.2: SAI >75 for 48h continuous -- not yet breached, as this was a second spike event after the 6-hour breach on March 2-3) and VantaFuel (clause 4.1: instantaneous SAI >80 triggers review -- BREACHED at 82). See DRIFT-20260306-001 for VantaFuel escalation.
- **Financial**: Combined sponsor risk exposure across all contracts with SAI clauses: $18.4M/yr in sponsorship revenue at contractual risk. VantaFuel ($12M/yr) was the most exposed due to its instantaneous-peak clause. OrionTel ($8.5M/yr) was approaching cumulative duration thresholds.
- **Brand reputation**: 340 media articles published in 48 hours referencing the Eclipse Dominion service issues. Dexerto ran a front-page story: "Eclipse Dominion's Tournament Disaster: Pro Players Threaten Boycott." The article was the #1 trending gaming story on Google News for 8 hours.
- **Investor relations**: AetherForge's parent company saw a 2.3% stock price decline ($180M market cap reduction) on March 5, attributed by analyst coverage to the Eclipse Dominion negative press cycle.

## Root Cause

The SAI spike was driven by media amplification of the competitive fairness complaints cascade (DRIFT-20260305-001). The pro player boycott threat, posted March 4 at 22:00 UTC, served as an amplification trigger that converted player-level frustration into a media narrative. The 14-hour delay between the boycott threat (22:00 UTC Mar 4) and the SAI peak (14:30 UTC Mar 5) corresponds to the typical media amplification cycle: initial social media virality (0-4h), esports journalist coverage (4-8h), mainstream gaming media pickup (8-14h).

The SAI's composite weighting meant that even as Discord ticket velocity began declining by March 5 morning (as the support team cleared the backlog), the Reddit and Twitter components continued climbing as the media narrative expanded beyond the original player base to the broader gaming community.

## Cascade Chain

- **Upstream**: DRIFT-20260305-001 (competitive fairness complaints and pro boycott threat drove media amplification)
- **Downstream**: DRIFT-20260306-001 (VantaFuel invoked instantaneous SAI clause at 82)
- **Decision trigger**: DLR-20260306-004 (executive decision to commission sponsor contract SAI clause renegotiation)

## Patch / Resolution

1. **Immediate sentiment response** (Mar 5, 15:00 UTC): Accelerated publication of the CEO/CTO co-signed transparency blog post (drafted for DRIFT-20260305-001 response). Amplified across all official channels. Paid promotion on Twitter/X to ensure visibility.
2. **Community management surge** (Mar 5-6): Deployed full community management team (18 staff across LA, Montreal, and Berlin) for 48-hour sustained engagement across Reddit, Discord, and Twitter. Response strategy: acknowledge, empathize, share specific technical remediation timelines.
3. **Media relations** (Mar 5, 18:00 UTC): VP Communications conducted media briefings with Dexerto, Dot Esports, TheGamer, and IGN, providing exclusive access to the server health dashboard (launching Mar 6) and the competitive integrity remediation plan.
4. **Proactive sponsor communication** (Mar 5, 16:00 UTC): VP Partnerships briefed all Tier-1 sponsors (OrionTel, VantaFuel, Nexwave, CyberDyne) on the situation, remediation timeline, and projected SAI recovery curve.
5. **SAI trajectory**: SAI declined from 82 (14:30 UTC Mar 5) to 68 (01:30 UTC Mar 6) to 54 (Mar 7) to 38 (Mar 9, post-Invitational finals). The transparency blog post and server health dashboard were credited by the analytics team as the primary drivers of the recovery (estimated -12 SAI points from proactive communication, -8 from media narrative shift).
