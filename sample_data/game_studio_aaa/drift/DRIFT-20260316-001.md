# DRIFT-20260316-001: Rollback Kill Switch Invoked: MTX

| Field | Value |
|---|---|

| **Reporter** | Monetization Systems Lead (Berlin) |
| **Studio** | Berlin, LA |

## Signal

On 2026-03-16 at 06:42 UTC, the EU MTX pricing kill switch was invoked in production for the first time in Eclipse Dominion's operational history. The kill switch -- a server-side configuration flag that reverts all EU storefront prices to a pre-defined snapshot -- was activated by the Berlin monetization systems team to roll back an inadvertent EU price increase that had propagated through the Season 4.1 patch deployment pipeline.

The root issue: the 4.1 patch (cosmetic bundle conversion, per DLR-20260311-001) included a pricing manifest that referenced the Season 4 price table instead of the post-rollback Season 3 price table. This meant that for approximately 3 hours and 17 minutes (03:25 UTC to 06:42 UTC), 14 of the 23 newly converted direct-purchase bundles were listed at Season 4 prices -- 15-22% above the rolled-back levels. During this window, 4,218 EU transactions were processed at the incorrect (higher) prices, totaling $87,400 in overcharges.

The kill switch activation reverted all EU prices to the `eu-mtx-s3-final` snapshot within 47 seconds of invocation. No player-facing downtime occurred. However, the incident required an additional refund cycle for the 4,218 affected transactions ($87,400 + $14,200 in payment processor fees).

## Impact

- **Financial**: $87,400 in overcharges requiring refund. $14,200 in payment processor fees. Total direct cost: $101,600. Minor relative to revenue but significant as a process failure signal.
- **Player trust**: 4,218 players saw incorrect prices. Of these, 1,340 submitted support tickets before the kill switch was activated. Community forums logged 89 new threads about "AetherForge raising prices again" within the 3-hour window. Damage was contained by rapid kill switch response and proactive refund communication.
- **Operational precedent**: First production kill switch invocation validates the kill switch design (DLR-20260309-001) but also reveals that the pricing manifest pipeline lacks a pre-deployment validation gate.
- **Regulatory risk**: Given the active EU DSC notice (DRIFT-20260309-001), any appearance of EU price manipulation -- even accidental -- increases regulatory scrutiny. AetherForge's EU legal counsel flagged this as a "near-miss" that could undermine the compliance narrative.

## Root Cause

The pricing manifest pipeline uses a YAML configuration file (`eu-pricing-manifest.yaml`) that specifies which price table snapshot to load. During the 4.1 patch build, the Montreal release engineer copied the manifest template from the Season 4 branch (pre-rollback) instead of the post-rollback branch. The build pipeline has no automated validation step to compare the pricing manifest against the active price table snapshot. The error was caught by Berlin's automated price monitoring alert, which triggers when any EU SKU price changes by more than 5% without an associated DLR.

- **Upstream**: [DLR-20260309-001](../decisions/DLR-20260309-001.md) -- Emergency MTX rollback (established the kill switch mechanism). [DRIFT-20260310-001](DRIFT-20260310-001.md) -- MTX refund rate spike (context for heightened sensitivity).
- **Downstream**: [DRIFT-20260317-001](DRIFT-20260317-001.md) -- Cross-region pricing parity drift (the EU rollback created a price gap vs NA that persisted).
- **Process improvement**: Triggered DLR-20260316-001 to add pricing manifest validation to the build pipeline.

## Patch / Resolution

Resolved within 4 hours via three actions:

1. **Kill switch activation** (2026-03-16 06:42 UTC): Reverted all EU prices to `eu-mtx-s3-final` snapshot. 47-second execution time. No player-facing downtime.
2. **Automated refund** (2026-03-16 10:00 UTC): All 4,218 affected transactions identified via transaction log diff. Refunds processed automatically with personalized apology message and 500 Eclipse Coins bonus (value: $4.99 equivalent). Total refund cost: $101,600.
3. **Pipeline fix** (2026-03-17): DLR-20260316-001 authorized addition of a pricing manifest validation gate to the build pipeline. The gate compares the manifest's referenced price table snapshot against the active production snapshot and blocks deployment if they diverge without an associated DLR. Deployed to CI/CD pipeline on 2026-03-18.
