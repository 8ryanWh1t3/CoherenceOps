## Context

Following the Emergency MTX Rollback EU (DLR-20260309-001) and the subsequent MTX refund rate spike captured in DRIFT-20260310-001, EU refund rates climbed to 6.8% -- more than triple the pre-crisis baseline of 1.9%. Apple and Google issued formal warnings that sustained refund rates above 5% would trigger enhanced review of Eclipse Dominion's storefront listings, risking temporary delisting in EU App Store and Google Play. APAC refund rates rose sympathetically to 3.4%, driven by social media contagion and JP player forums amplifying EU grievances. The existing refund policy (14-day no-questions-asked for purchases over EUR 9.99) was drafted pre-launch and never stress-tested against a monetization crisis of this magnitude.

## Options Considered

1. **Revised tiered refund policy with automated processing (selected)** -- purchases under $4.99 auto-refund within 72h; $5-$19.99 within 48h with one-click flow; $20+ within 24h with priority queue. All regions.
2. **EU-only emergency refund extension** -- extend refund window to 30 days for EU only. Lower implementation cost but creates regional disparity and does not address APAC.
3. **Status quo with manual escalation team** -- hire 12 temporary CS agents to handle refund backlog. Band-aid that does not address root cause or platform holder concerns.
4. **Full refund amnesty for 90 days** -- blanket refund for any purchase in the crisis window. Highest goodwill but projected $4.2M write-off.

## Decision

Adopt Option 1. Implement a tiered automated refund policy across EU and APAC regions, with NA and LATAM aligned within 14 days. Automated processing eliminates the CS bottleneck that caused 72h average response times during the crisis. Tier thresholds calibrated to Apple/Google safe-harbor guidelines (sub-3% refund rate target).

## Trade-offs

- (+) Reduces average refund processing time from 72h to <6h for sub-$5 purchases.
- (+) Brings refund rate within Apple/Google safe harbor, eliminating delisting risk.
- (+) Automated flow reduces CS headcount requirement by 8 FTEs (~$520K/yr).
- (-) Auto-refund on sub-$4.99 items increases projected refund volume by ~11% short-term.
- (-) Engineering cost of $185K for payment gateway integration and refund automation pipeline.
- (-) APAC refund window change requires renegotiation with Sony PlayStation Asia (est. 3 weeks).

## Kill Switch / Rollback

Revert to manual refund processing with pre-crisis policy. Automated refund pipeline includes a feature flag (`refund-auto-tier-v2`) that can be disabled per-region within 15 minutes. Manual CS queue resumes with existing staff. Rollback time: <30 minutes per region.
