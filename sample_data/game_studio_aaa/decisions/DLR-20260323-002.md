## Context

Q2 2026 capacity planning must account for the dramatic CCU variance observed during W09-W12. The tournament spike (W10) drove peak CCU to 2.1M -- 340% above projections (ASM-0005, now expired). The monetization backlash (W11) caused EU DAU to drop 18%, while NA and APAC remained stable. W12 telemetry shows early recovery signals: EU DAU rebounding at +3.2%/week, NA steady at 1.1M peak CCU, APAC growing at 2.8%/week post-latency improvements (DLR-20260304-001). ASM-0010 assumes current growth rates are sufficient for Q2 provisioning, but the assumption was set pre-crisis and needs validation against actual W12 data. Season 4 mid-season tournament (scheduled W18) is expected to produce another CCU spike, though likely smaller than W10 given improved infrastructure (DLR-20260316-003).

## Options Considered

1. **Tiered capacity plan with auto-scaling headroom (selected)** -- provision base capacity at 1.5x steady-state per region, with auto-scaling configured to burst to 3x for tournament events. Pre-warm instances 24h before scheduled events.
2. **Flat 2x over-provision all regions** -- simple but expensive; $2.8M/yr in idle capacity.
3. **Reactive auto-scaling only** -- rely entirely on cloud auto-scaling with no pre-provisioning. Lowest base cost but cold-start risk during spikes (W10 showed 4-minute scale-up lag).
4. **Hybrid: reserved instances NA/EU + spot APAC** -- reserved for predictable regions, spot for APAC growth. Cost-effective but spot interruption risk in APAC during peak.

## Decision

Adopt Option 1. Provision Q2 capacity as follows:
- **NA**: Base 1.4M CCU (1.5x steady-state 940K); burst to 2.8M for events.
- **EU**: Base 1.1M CCU (1.5x steady-state 720K); burst to 2.2M. Conservative due to recovery uncertainty.
- **APAC**: Base 680K CCU (1.5x steady-state 450K); burst to 1.8M. Aggressive headroom for growth trajectory.

Auto-scaling triggers at 65% utilization (down from 80% pre-crisis). Pre-warm protocol: 24h before any scheduled event, spin up burst capacity to 50% and hold. Monthly capacity review aligned with weekly telemetry cadence.

## Trade-offs

- (+) 1.5x base provides comfortable headroom without W10-level over-spend.
- (+) Pre-warm protocol eliminates cold-start risk observed in W10.
- (+) Regional tuning accounts for EU recovery uncertainty vs APAC growth.
- (-) Annual cost of $1.92M (up from $1.35M in Q1 plan).
- (-) Aggressive APAC provisioning may be wasted if growth plateaus.
- (-) Monthly review cadence may miss rapid changes between reviews.

## Assumptions

- **ASM-0010**: Q2 server capacity sufficient at current growth rate (validated with W12 data adjustments).
- **ASM-0005**: Tournament CCU peak 620K (expired; replaced with 2.1M actual from W10 for planning baseline).

## Kill Switch / Rollback

Reduce to 1.2x base provisioning (Q1 levels) via Terraform variable change. Auto-scaling burst limits remain in place as safety net. Rollback time: <20 minutes per region (Terraform apply). Pre-warm protocol can be disabled independently.
