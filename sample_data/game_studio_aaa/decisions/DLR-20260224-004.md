## Context

Eclipse Dominion has 47 weapons across 6 classes. The current manual balance process relies on designer intuition and community feedback cycles of 2-4 weeks. Season 3 saw 3 "meta-warping" weapons dominate ranked play for 5+ weeks before patches, driving a 4.1% drop in ranked participation. A data-driven framework is needed to detect and respond to balance outliers within days, not weeks.

## Options Considered

1. **Data-driven balance framework with automated anomaly detection (selected)** -- telemetry-fed dashboards flag weapons exceeding statistical thresholds; designers validate and push tuning changes.
2. **Community-driven balance council** -- weekly votes from top-500 ranked players. Low cost but biased toward high-skill meta.
3. **Faster manual cycle** -- hire 2 additional balance designers, shorten patch cycle to weekly. Incremental improvement, doesn't scale.

## Decision

Adopt Option 1. Build automated balance telemetry pipeline (Montreal). Flag any weapon with pick-rate >2 standard deviations from class mean or win-delta >4% from 50% baseline. Designers review flagged weapons within 48h. Tuning changes deployed via server-side hotfix.

## Trade-offs

- (+) Detection-to-patch cycle reduced from ~3 weeks to ~4 days.
- (+) Objective data replaces subjective designer intuition for initial detection.
- (-) Risk of over-tuning if thresholds are too sensitive (churn from constant meta shifts).
- (-) Does not capture "feel" issues that data alone cannot surface.

## Assumptions

- **ASM-0011**: Montreal and LA can share a single telemetry pipeline without latency issues for balance data.

## Success Metrics

| Metric                              | Target       | Baseline (S3) |
|-------------------------------------|--------------|---------------|
| TTK variance across weapon classes  | <8%          | 14.2%         |
| Meta-warping weapon duration        | <7 days      | 35+ days      |
| Ranked participation retention      | >92% monthly | 87.6%         |
| Balance patch cadence               | Weekly       | Bi-weekly     |

## Kill Switch / Rollback

Revert to v1 balance tables stored in `balance/v1-archive/`. Server-side rollback within 30 minutes. Automated detection system can be disabled independently without affecting balance values.
