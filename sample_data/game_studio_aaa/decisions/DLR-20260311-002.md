## Context

Between Weeks 10 and 11, the Eclipse Dominion competitive community escalated allegations that premium loadouts (purchasable via MTX) provide measurable competitive advantage in ranked play. Specific claims: the "Vanguard Elite" weapon skin bundle includes a scope with marginally better ADS speed (+3 frames), and the "Phantom Core" armor set provides a hitbox that is 2.1% smaller than the default model. Community data miners published frame-by-frame comparisons viewed 1.8M times on social media. VantaFuel's sponsorship contract (clause 4.1) includes an explicit pay-to-win suspension trigger -- if AetherForge's game is "reasonably determined to provide purchasable competitive advantage," VantaFuel may suspend the $12M/yr sponsorship with 30 days' notice.

## Options Considered

1. **Independent audit + competitive parity mode (selected)** -- commission a third-party audit of all premium items for competitive impact, publish methodology and results, and implement a "Competitive Parity" mode for ranked play where all cosmetic items are normalized to default hitbox and animation timings.
2. **Internal review + silent fix** -- AetherForge QA audits and silently patches any discrepancies. Low cost but does not address community trust deficit.
3. **Blanket cosmetic normalization** -- force all players to default models in ranked. Eliminates the problem but destroys MTX value proposition for competitive players.
4. **Community advisory board** -- form a player-led review board for competitive item fairness. Good PR but slow and does not address immediate VantaFuel risk.

## Decision

Adopt Option 1. Execute a three-part response:

1. **Independent Audit** (begin 2026-03-14) -- engage Digital Fairness Labs (DFL), a third-party game integrity firm, to audit all 147 premium items for competitive impact. Audit scope: hitbox geometry, animation timing, visual effects (including muzzle flash occlusion), audio cues. Publish full methodology and results publicly.
2. **Competitive Parity Mode** (ship 2026-03-25) -- new ranked mode option where all players render with standardized hitboxes and animation timings regardless of equipped cosmetics. Cosmetic visuals remain (players see their skins) but server-side calculations use default geometry.
3. **Interim Fix** (hotfix 2026-03-13) -- normalize the two specific items flagged by community (Vanguard Elite ADS, Phantom Core hitbox) to default values immediately.

## Trade-offs

- (+) Independent audit provides credible evidence for VantaFuel and community.
- (+) Competitive Parity mode is a permanent structural fix.
- (+) Interim fix demonstrates immediate responsiveness.
- (-) Total cost: $340K (audit) + $220K (Competitive Parity dev) = $560K.
- (-) Publishing audit results may reveal additional discrepancies beyond the two flagged items.
- (-) Competitive Parity mode may reduce perceived value of premium cosmetics in ranked play.
- (-) Normalizing Vanguard Elite and Phantom Core may anger players who purchased them for the advantage.

## Assumptions

- **ASM-0010**: Premium cosmetic items do not provide competitive advantage. This assumption is now under formal audit. If invalidated, broader MTX policy review is required.
