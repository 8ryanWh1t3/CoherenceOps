## Context

The Emergency MTX Rollback EU (DLR-20260309-001) reversed a 35% cosmetic price increase that had triggered regulatory scrutiny and a 6.8% refund rate. While the EU rollback stabilized that region, DRIFT-20260317-001 identified a cross-region pricing parity drift: NA prices remained at the elevated tier while EU reverted, creating a 35% price gap for identical items. NA community forums surfaced the discrepancy within 48h, generating 6,800 negative posts and a Change.org petition (22K signatures) demanding global price alignment. APAC pricing was never increased but sat 15% below NA baseline, creating a three-tier pricing inconsistency. With EU DAU recovering (+3.2%/week per TELEMETRY-W12) and NA churn stable but sentiment deteriorating, a global pricing rebalance is needed before the W13 recovery window closes and the pricing gap becomes entrenched in player expectations.

## Options Considered

1. **Global rebalance to pre-increase baseline + regional PPP adjustment (selected)** -- revert all regions to pre-February baseline, then apply purchasing power parity (PPP) adjustments: NA baseline, EU -5%, APAC -12%, LATAM -22%.
2. **NA rollback to match EU** -- simply revert NA to match EU pricing. Fast but ignores APAC/LATAM and leaves PPP unaddressed.
3. **Premium tier restructure** -- replace flat pricing with tiered bundles (Good/Better/Best). Addresses value perception but 8-week implementation; too slow for recovery window.
4. **Temporary discount event** -- 30-day "Eclipse Appreciation" sale at 25% off globally. Quick goodwill but trains players to wait for sales; damages ARPPU long-term.

## Decision

Adopt Option 1. Execute a global cosmetic pricing rebalance effective 2026-03-28 (coinciding with Season 4 Week 5 content drop for maximum positive framing). All cosmetic items revert to pre-February 2026 baseline pricing with PPP regional adjustments:
- **NA**: Baseline (no change from pre-increase).
- **EU**: -5% from NA baseline (reflects EUR/USD parity + regulatory sensitivity).
- **APAC**: -12% from NA baseline (reflects JPY/USD PPP + market growth investment).
- **LATAM**: -22% from NA baseline (reflects BRL/USD PPP).

Communicated as "Eclipse Dominion Global Fair Pricing Initiative" -- framed as proactive, not reactive.

## Trade-offs

- (+) Eliminates cross-region pricing parity drift permanently.
- (+) PPP adjustment acknowledges regional purchasing power; expected to improve APAC/LATAM conversion rates.
- (+) Timing with W13 content drop creates positive narrative; recovery accelerator.
- (-) NA net revenue impact of -$1.8M/yr (revert from elevated prices).
- (-) Global ARPPU expected to drop 8% short-term before volume compensates.
- (-) PPP adjustments may invite arbitrage via VPN (mitigated by payment region lock).

## Assumptions

- **ASM-0004**: Battle Pass ARPPU $14.50 baseline -- this rebalance targets $13.80 post-adjustment with volume offset.
- **ASM-0011**: Direct purchase conversion rate >60% of loot box (EU) -- pricing must support this transition.

## Kill Switch / Rollback

Revert to pre-rebalance pricing per region via storefront config push. Each region independently controllable. Feature flag: `pricing-rebalance-v2-{region}`. Rollback time: <30 minutes per region (storefront cache refresh).
