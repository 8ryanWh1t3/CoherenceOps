# DLR-20260224-002: Server Infrastructure Scaling Policy

## Context

Eclipse Dominion averages 1.2M DAU with peaks of 2.1M during content drops and tournaments. Current manual scaling has caused three P1 outages in the last two quarters, each lasting 25-90 minutes and costing an estimated $2.7M per incident in lost revenue and sponsor penalty clauses. A formal auto-scaling policy with circuit breakers is required before the EDCS W10 tournament window.

## Options Considered

1. **Threshold-based auto-scaling with circuit breakers (selected)** -- define CPU/connection thresholds, automatic scale-out with hard ceiling, circuit breaker at saturation.
2. **Predictive ML-based scaling** -- train model on historical load patterns. Higher accuracy but 4-month lead time.
3. **Over-provision static capacity at 3x peak** -- simple but annual cost +$9.6M.

## Decision

Adopt Option 1. Auto-scale at 70% CPU / 80% connection-pool utilization. Circuit breaker trips at 95% capacity, activating queue-based admission control. Hard ceiling at 4x baseline to prevent runaway spend. Manual override available at 150% capacity threshold.

## Trade-offs

- (+) Eliminates manual scaling response lag (currently 12-18 min).
- (+) Circuit breaker prevents cascading failures during extreme spikes.
- (-) Capex of $3.2M for additional reserved capacity and tooling.
- (-) False positive circuit breaker trips may briefly queue legitimate players.

## Assumptions

- **ASM-0002**: Average player session length remains ~42 min (affects connection pool sizing).
- **ASM-0005**: Peak concurrent users will not exceed 2.5M before Q3 2026.
- **ASM-0013**: Cloud provider (AWS) can deliver requested instance types within 90s in us-east-1, ap-northeast-1, eu-central-1.

## Success Metrics

| Metric                      | Target        | Baseline      |
|-----------------------------|---------------|---------------|
| P1 outages from scaling     | 0 per quarter | 1.5 per quarter|
| Scale-out response time     | <120s         | 12-18 min     |
| Player queue wait (p99)     | <30s          | N/A (outage)  |

## Kill Switch / Rollback

Manual scaling override at 150% capacity. Disable auto-scaler via infrastructure flag `SCALING_POLICY=manual`. Rollback to manual runbook within 15 minutes.
