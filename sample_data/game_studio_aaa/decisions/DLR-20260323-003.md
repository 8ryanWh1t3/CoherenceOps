## Context

The Competitive Fairness Protest Response (DLR-20260306-004) and the Tournament Balance Patch Freeze (DLR-20260302-003) exposed a structural gap: balance patches ship to 28M players simultaneously with no competitive community validation. During W10, the Nyx Dagger range nerf (Patch 4.2.1) inverted the ranked meta overnight, triggering a 340% spike in competitive fairness complaints (DRIFT-20260305-001) and a Reddit megathread with 12K upvotes demanding reversal. EVID-0004 (Ranked Fairness Player Survey) confirmed that 71% of Diamond+ players want advance notice of balance changes. CLAIM-0008 projects that a preview program could reduce post-patch complaints by 40%. The esports community (top 0.5% of ranked players, ~140K accounts) has disproportionate influence: they generate 38% of streaming content and drive 22% of new player acquisition through viewership.

## Options Considered

1. **Top-ranked preview program with structured feedback (selected)** -- invite top 2,000 ranked players to a PTR (Public Test Realm) 72h before each balance patch. Structured feedback via survey + telemetry. NDA required.
2. **Full public PTR** -- open PTR to all players. Maximum coverage but spoils patch surprise; data-mining leaks inevitable.
3. **Pro player advisory council** -- invite 20 professional players for private feedback sessions. High-quality signal but tiny sample; perception of favoritism.
4. **AI-driven balance simulation** -- use ML models to simulate meta impact. Promising but 6+ months to build; does not address community engagement gap.

## Decision

Adopt Option 1. Launch a Balance Patch Preview Program (BPPP) on the Eclipse Dominion PTR. Top 2,000 players by ranked MMR (Diamond III and above) receive 72h early access to each balance patch. Participants must accept an NDA and complete a structured feedback survey (15 questions, ~8 min). Telemetry from PTR sessions feeds directly into the balance team's adjustment model. Montreal Gameplay Systems owns patch content; LA Esports Ops manages the PTR logistics and community engagement.

## Trade-offs

- (+) Catches meta-breaking changes before they hit live servers.
- (+) Builds goodwill with competitive community; perceived investment in fairness.
- (+) Structured telemetry provides quantitative balance data from high-skill play.
- (-) 72h delay in patch deployment cadence (accepted as cost of quality).
- (-) NDA enforcement is soft; leaks to Reddit/Discord likely within 24h.
- (-) Top-2K selection may alienate mid-tier ranked players who feel excluded.

## Assumptions

- **CLAIM-0008**: Preview program reduces post-patch complaints by 40%.
- **DLR-20260302-003**: Balance patch freeze protocol remains in effect during tournament windows.

## Kill Switch / Rollback

Disable PTR access and revert to direct-to-live patch deployment. PTR environment remains available for internal QA. NDA obligations persist for 90 days post-program suspension. Rollback time: immediate (PTR access revocation).
