## Context

Eclipse Dominion's Season 3 Battle Pass generated $47.2M globally. Market analysis shows competitor titles increased pass pricing by 8-15% in NA/EU without material churn. APAC and LATAM markets remain price-sensitive with lower ARPU. AetherForge's $480M annual revenue target requires incremental monetization gains without alienating the 6.8M active pass holders.

## Options Considered

1. **10% increase NA/EU, flat APAC/LATAM (selected)** -- regional pricing; maximizes revenue in high-ARPU markets while protecting growth regions.
2. **Flat 7% global increase** -- simpler but risks 5-6% churn in price-sensitive regions.
3. **Tiered premium pass** -- add $19.99 "Elite" tier above standard $9.99. Higher ceiling but cannibalizes standard pass.
4. **Hold pricing, increase pass content** -- protects goodwill but no revenue uplift.

## Decision

Adopt Option 1. Increase NA Battle Pass from $9.99 to $10.99, EU from EUR 9.99 to EUR 10.99. Hold APAC and LATAM at current local-currency pricing. Effective Season 4 launch (2026-03-15).

## Trade-offs

- (+) Projected +$4.8M quarterly revenue uplift from NA/EU alone.
- (+) No disruption to APAC/LATAM growth trajectory.
- (-) Expected 2.8-3.2% churn among NA/EU pass holders (modeled, not observed).
- (-) Community sentiment risk; requires proactive content-value messaging.

## Assumptions

- **ASM-0004**: Price elasticity -- 10% price increase yields ~3% churn in NA/EU (based on Season 2 A/B test data).
- **ASM-0007**: Season 4 content volume is at least 15% greater than Season 3 (justifies price increase narrative).

## Kill Switch / Rollback

Revert pricing within 48 hours via server-side config push. No client update required. Price rollback automatically triggers "loyalty bonus" 200 premium currency credit to affected purchasers.
