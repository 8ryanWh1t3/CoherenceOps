## Context

On 2026-03-08, AetherForge received formal notice from the EU Digital Services Coordinator regarding Eclipse Dominion's Season 4 microtransaction pricing structure (implemented per DLR-20260224-003). Simultaneously, EU refund requests spiked +340% over 72 hours and EU DAU dropped 18% week-over-week. The pricing changes -- which increased average MTX cost by 22% in EU markets -- were originally projected to yield +$8.4M annual EU revenue (ASM-0006) and assumed EU regulatory tolerance for dynamic pricing (ASM-0014). Both assumptions are now invalidated by observed data.

This decision is the **kill switch activation** for DLR-20260224-003.

## Options Considered

1. **Full rollback to pre-Season 4 EU pricing (selected)** -- immediate reversion of all EU MTX prices to Season 3 levels.
2. **Partial rollback (cosmetics only)** -- revert cosmetic pricing, maintain battle pass pricing. Reduces financial impact but may not satisfy regulator.
3. **Negotiate with DSC** -- engage legal counsel to contest the notice. Timeline: 6-12 weeks. Risk: DAU continues to bleed.
4. **EU storefront suspension** -- take EU store offline entirely until resolution. Nuclear option.

## Decision

Adopt Option 1. Immediately roll back all EU microtransaction prices to pre-Season 4 levels (as of 2026-02-23). Pricing database snapshot `eu-mtx-s3-final` is the restoration target. Execute within 6 hours of this decision. Issue public statement acknowledging the reversion and committing to player-first pricing. Refund all EU purchases made at Season 4 prices above the Season 3 equivalent (estimated 128,000 transactions, ~$2.1M in refunds).

## Trade-offs

- (+) Immediate compliance with EU DSC notice -- avoids formal investigation.
- (+) Stops DAU hemorrhage in EU market.
- (+) Demonstrates regulatory good faith.
- (-) Quarterly EU MTX revenue impact of -$4.8M.
- (-) Refund processing costs ~$2.1M + $340K in payment processor fees.
- (-) Invalidates the financial projections that justified DLR-20260224-003.
- (-) Reputational damage from visible price reversal.

## Assumptions

- **ASM-0006**: ~~EU players will accept up to 25% MTX price increase with sufficient cosmetic value.~~ INVALIDATED. Actual tolerance was <10% based on refund and churn data.
- **ASM-0014**: ~~EU regulatory environment tolerates dynamic pricing for cosmetic-only microtransactions.~~ INVALIDATED by DSC notice.
