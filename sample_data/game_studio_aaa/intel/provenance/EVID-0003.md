## 1. Summary

Week 11 (2026-03-09 through 2026-03-15) saw the highest sustained negative player
sentiment since Eclipse Dominion's Season 2 launch. The Sentiment Anomaly Index
(SAI) peaked at **82** on 2026-03-12, driven by the convergence of the EU MTX price
increase, a weapon-balance controversy ("Vortex Rifle" meta dominance), and delayed
hotfix response. This document details the methodology, raw signals, and correlation
with retention metrics.

## 2. SAI Methodology

The Sentiment Anomaly Index is a composite score (0--100) computed from three
weighted sub-signals:

| Sub-Signal              | Source       | Weight | Method                              |
|-------------------------|-------------|--------|--------------------------------------|
| Social NLP Negativity   | Reddit, Twitter | 0.45   | VADER + custom fine-tuned DistilBERT |
| Discord Report Volume   | #bug-reports, #feedback | 0.30 | Ticket count z-score vs. 4-week MA  |
| In-Game Feedback        | Pause-menu survey | 0.25 | 1-5 star rating, inverted and scaled |

**SAI = 0.45(NLP_z) + 0.30(Discord_z) + 0.25(InGame_z), scaled to 0--100.**

Baseline SAI during stable weeks (W06--W09) averaged **23** with a standard deviation
of 7.

## 4. Sub-Signal Breakdown at Peak (2026-03-12)

### 4.1 Social NLP (Reddit + Twitter)

- **Reddit r/EclipseDominion:** 2,847 posts analyzed. Negative sentiment share: 71%
  (baseline: 22%). Top negative topics: "price gouge" (34%), "Vortex broken" (28%),
  "devs don't care" (18%).
- **Twitter #EclipseDominion:** 14,200 tweets in 24h (baseline: 3,100). Negative
  sentiment share: 64%. Viral thread by @ProGamer_Kira (1.2M impressions) titled
  "Eclipse Dominion is dead to me" drove 23% of negative volume.
- **NLP Model:** VADER for real-time scoring; DistilBERT fine-tuned on 12K labeled
  gaming-sentiment examples for nuanced classification. Inter-annotator agreement
  (Cohen's kappa): 0.81.

### 4.2 Discord Report Volume

- **#bug-reports:** 438 tickets (baseline 7-day avg: 89). 3.2 standard deviations
  above mean.
- **#feedback:** 612 messages (baseline: 145). 2.9 standard deviations above mean.
- **Dominant themes:** Refund requests (31%), weapon-balance complaints (27%),
  server-performance reports (19%), loot-box fairness (14%).

### 4.3 In-Game Feedback

- **Response rate:** 4.8% of session-ends triggered survey (baseline: 2.1%).
- **Mean rating:** 2.1 / 5.0 (baseline: 3.6 / 5.0).
- **Free-text analysis:** "expensive" appeared in 42% of responses; "broken" in 31%.

## 6. Limitations

- SAI is a lagging composite; real-time sub-signal spikes may precede composite
  movement by 6--12 hours.
- Twitter API rate limits caused ~8% sample loss during peak hours on 2026-03-12.
- In-game survey has self-selection bias (frustrated players more likely to respond).
- Correlation between SAI and D7 retention is observational; causal attribution
  requires controlled experiment.
- Small sample size for weekly correlation (n=8) limits statistical power.

## 7. Recommendations

1. Establish SAI **early-warning threshold at 50** (currently only alerting at 70).
2. Pre-draft community-response templates for top-5 negative-sentiment archetypes.
3. Implement automated "crisis mode" staffing escalation when SAI >60 for >4 hours.
4. Commission causal study (A/B holdout) to validate SAI-retention relationship.
5. Add Twitch/YouTube chat sentiment as a fourth sub-signal for tournament weekends.
