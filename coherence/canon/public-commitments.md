# Public Commitments

Promises made to users, adopters, or stakeholders. These are canon — changing them is a deliberate act.

## Commitments

### C-001: MIT License
CoherenceOps is and will remain MIT-licensed. Adopters can use, modify, and distribute freely.

### C-002: No External Dependencies
CoherenceOps will never require a SaaS account, database, or external service to function. GitHub is the only platform requirement.

### C-003: Backward-Compatible Templates
Template changes will be additive. Existing DLRs and drift records will remain valid across versions. We will not break the format without a major version bump and migration guide.

### C-004: Coherence Score Formula Is Public
The scoring formula is documented in `actions/COHERENCE_SCORE_SPEC.md` and will not change without a DLR and changelog entry.
