# Mission

## Statement

CoherenceOps exists to make institutional decision-making visible, versioned, and recoverable.

## Why This Matters

Software teams make hundreds of decisions. Most are invisible: buried in chat, lost in meetings, stored in one person's head. When that person leaves, the team loses not just a contributor but the *reasons* behind the system's shape.

CoherenceOps treats decisions as infrastructure — recorded, searchable, and subject to drift detection — so that "why did we build it this way?" always has an answer.

## Non-Goals

- CoherenceOps is not a project management tool
- CoherenceOps does not replace code review
- CoherenceOps does not require AI or external services
